Introduction

The website contains a collection of my posts, mainly in the format of mini-essays on an array of topics related to Ethics in Technology, Digitalization, and data management (Choice 1 tasks), or commentary on the memes/images that interact with the course material in some way (Choice 3 tasks).

What is this site's purpose?

This is supposed to be a sort of a portfolio showcasing my work in the ISTA 161 course. My goal is to assess, summarize, and meaningfully connect my knowledge on the topic of ethical Data Science practices, the issues of acquiring, preserving, and destroying data, as well as the ways it should be properly published/distributed, and explain what I have learned in this class.
