Week 3: Data Cleaning

Why Data Cleaning is Important

The focus of this week was on detecting, diagnosing, and correcting data abnormalities (including outliers). Discussions include trade-offs between correcting errors and documenting them, and why clean data is essential for trustworthy analysis. The data cleaning step must never be skipped, because a dataset with errrors in it leads to humorous (and not so much) erroneous research conclusions.

Finally, once the cleaning is over, we have to answer questions related to data use.
