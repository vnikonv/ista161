Ethical Ways of Collecting Data

This week covers how data is collected, the responsibilities around acquisition, and the ethical considerations when sourcing datasets.

The illustrations provided are my two visualizations from my "Data Analysis in Python using Pandas" class. The first one is a distribution of ratings by language, and the second one is a correlation between average rating and number of pages. Each dot in the second chart represents one book. I used the Goodreads dataset from Kaggle.
